Da Nang City

Geography

Da Nang City is a very beautiful place that is surrounded by mountains to the West and the South China Sea to the East. This city has a lot of gorgeous beaches and mountainous areas, so many tourists choose this city to be a must-try place on their trip to Viet Nam.

Climate

Because Viet Nam is a tropical country so in Da Nang, there are 2 main seasons: the typhoon and wet season from September to December and a dry season from January to August. The average temperature in here is around 26 oC to 30 oC.

Citizen

The people in Da Nang are really friendly and kind. When coming here, you will be surprised about the ways Da Nang's people cheat strangers. They are always willing to help foreigners and tourists to find the things they need with luminous smiles.

Bridges

Da Nang City has a lot of gorgeous bridges, such as:

  1. Dragon Bridge
  2. Han River Bridge
  3. Thuan Phuoc Bridge
